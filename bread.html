Description

Baking bread at home is a fulfilling culinary endeavor that fills your kitchen with the aroma of freshly baked goodness. To start, gather your ingredients: flour, yeast, water, salt, and optionally, sugar or honey for sweetness. In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, yeast, salt, and sugar or honey (if using). Slowly add warm water to the dry ingredients, mixing until a sticky dough forms. Knead the dough on a floured surface until it becomes smooth and elastic, about 8-10 minutes. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place until doubled in size, usually about 1-2 hours.

Once risen, punch down the dough to release air bubbles, then shape it into a loaf or rolls. Transfer the shaped dough to a baking sheet or loaf pan, cover, and let it rise again for another 30-60 minutes. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and bake the bread for 25-30 minutes, or until golden brown and hollow-sounding when tapped on the bottom. Allow the bread to cool on a wire rack before slicing and serving. Enjoy the warm, freshly baked bread with butter or your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Sugar or honey
  • Salt
  • Herbs

Steps to Follow

  1. Combine flour, yeast, salt, sugar and herbs in a mixing bowl.
  2. Add warm water gradually, mixing until dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on floured surface until smooth.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover and let it rese until doubled in size.
  5. Punch the dough down and shape it into loaf.
  6. Transfer into a pan and cover and let rise again for 30-60 minutes.
  7. Prehear the oven to 190 degree celcius and bake for 25-30 minutes.
  8. Allow the bread to cool before slicing and serving.
